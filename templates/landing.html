Key Features

Precise Detection

Powered by ResNet50 architecture to classify up to 6 different fabric defect types with exceptional accuracy. Our model has been trained on thousands of fabric samples to ensure reliable detection.

Visual Localization

Grad-CAM technology visually highlights exactly where the defect is located on the fabric, enabling quality control engineers to pinpoint issues quickly and efficiently.

AI Assistant

Integrated Gemini-powered chatbot to explain detected defects, suggest remedial actions, and provide expert guidance on textile quality control measures.

About This Project

FabricAI is an innovative solution designed to revolutionize textile quality control processes. By leveraging cutting-edge deep learning techniques, our system automates the traditionally manual and time-consuming process of fabric defect detection.

Built with state-of-the-art computer vision models and explainable AI technologies, FabricAI provides manufacturers with accurate, real-time defect analysis while offering insights into defect characteristics and potential solutions.

This project demonstrates the power of artificial intelligence in industrial applications, bridging the gap between academic research and practical implementation in manufacturing environments.
